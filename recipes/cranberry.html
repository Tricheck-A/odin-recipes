Maple Syrup Cranberry Sauce

DESCRIPTION

A wonderful Cranberry Sauce

Per Serving:
179 calories; protein 0.2g; carbohydrates 44.3g; fat 0.2g; sodium 7.7mg.


INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup maple syrup
  • 1 (12 ounce) bag fresh cranberries
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 dash lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon brandy

STEPS

  1. Step
    Combine water and maple syrup in a saucepan; bring to a boil. Add cranberries
    to syrup mixture and cook at a boil until berries pop, 10 to 15 minutes. Stir brown sugar
    and lemon juice into cranberries until brown sugar is completely dissolved. Mix in
    brandy.
